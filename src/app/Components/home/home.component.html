First of all, one of the most important features of our application is that its very easy to use, in fact our user who want to sell a product can post it with specific images, price, and all the needed details to promote it so we can ensure diversity and unicity even with a great number of vendors . Second of all, our application garantee a diverse selection of products, in fact while developing its code and its architecture we made sure that we are not limited to just one type of products but we include a fonctionalty with which you can search products by the category you are interested in or by name and that can save you a lot of time.

Also, one of the most important features of Consommi Tounsi is that it goes both ways, what i mean is our users can be both vendors or customers they can either post their products and promote it or they can choose from our gallery and buy the needed items. One more thing, is that Consommi Tounsi platform is both resourceful and valuable, indeed our users can participate to cultural and various events thanks to it, not to forget to mention that they can also support humanitarian causes by donating or sponsoring various charity philanthropic events. Last but not least, one of our important features is that you can wether encourage or criticize by rating and commenting sellers and their products in order to ensure the evolution and the development.
